Our Mission

The Problem

Electronic waste is the fastest-growing waste stream in the world, with over 50 million metric tons generated annually. This e-waste contains hazardous materials that can leach into soil and water, posing serious environmental and health risks.

Our Solution

IWB PTY LTD provides comprehensive e-waste management solutions that ensure responsible recycling, secure data destruction, and material recovery. We turn electronic waste into valuable resources while protecting our environment.
